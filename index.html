Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04 December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Cyberpunk Calculator, developed by SDS Dev. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

Cyberpunk Calculator does NOT collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information.

We do not collect:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files
  • IP address
  • Usage analytics

The app works entirely offline.

3. Local Data Storage

The app stores only two types of data, and both remain completely on your device:

  • Calculator History – saved locally for your convenience
  • App Settings (theme mode, text size) – stored using SharedPreferences

No data is uploaded to any server.

4. Permissions

Cyberpunk Calculator does not request access to:

  • Storage
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Location

The app functions without any special permissions.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is safe for all ages. Since we do not collect any data, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Data Security

All data stays on your device and never leaves it. This ensures complete privacy and security.

7. Your Control

You can erase all local data at any time by:

  • Clearing history from within the app
  • Reinstalling the application

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page.
